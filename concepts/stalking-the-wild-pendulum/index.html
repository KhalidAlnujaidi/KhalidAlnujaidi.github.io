Stalking the Wild Pendulum Itzhak Bentov — on the mechanics of consciousness

Bentov argued that reality is vibration, resonance, and coupled fields — matter mostly empty space threaded with oscillating energy, bodies as coupled oscillators in one planetary medium. Those ideas are spatial and dynamic, so here each chapter is a small museum of interactive concepts: drag a slider, orbit the scene, and watch the abstraction become something you can feel.
